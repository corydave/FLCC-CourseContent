# Topics for this chapter

This chapter is a recap of some of the fundamental math you'll need to know before embarking on your geometric adventures! We'll look at **decimals** and **fractions** (as well as performing mathematical operations with them)

* Converting fractions to decimals and decimals to fractions

* Improper fractions, repeating fractions, and irrational numbers

* Ratios

* Reducing fractions

* Adding, subtracting, multiplying, and dividing fractions and decimals




# To ponder

Before starting this chapter, see if you can answer these questions:
1. What is a *leading zero*?

2. What is an *improper fraction*?

3. What is the value of &pi;?
