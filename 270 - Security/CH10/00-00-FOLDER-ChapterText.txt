For a long time, there were very few laws that regulated cybercrime. In fact, the most influential law governing computers was written in 1986 - almost a decade before widespread internet adoption! That was the Computer Fraud and Abuse Act. We'll be looking at organizations, laws, and thought-leaders in the industry. And how that landscape is rapidly changing.
