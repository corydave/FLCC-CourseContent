[BookCreator](https://bookcreator.com/) is a neat piece of software that lets you (or your students) create a slick digital book. 

Some educators might use this in the classroom by having their students make books the site is easy enough for kids to use. But it is also sophisticated enough that you can create pretty sweet looking books for your kiddos.

You can see a sample book below.

Your job is to make a sweet, sweet book. The topic can be anything you want, but ideally would be something you might use in your classroom. Check the specifications below for all the things it should include.

Note that when you go to create a book, you can choose from a few different sizes and templates. If you choose the Comic Book theme you'll have all the regular tools *and* some extra, special tools as well.
