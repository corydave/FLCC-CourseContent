^^ PODCAST - Landing your Dream Job

The [New Way We Work](https://www.fastcompany.com/podcasts/new-way-we-work) is a podcast from Fast Company. If you are seriously interested in finding the best career for you and you want tips from professionals, look through the catalog of this podcast and pick a few that interest you.

One interesting episode from July, 2023 includes an interview with Judith Humphrey, author of the book "The Job Seeker's Script." In the interview she gives great advice on job searching, buffing your resume, and cover letters.
