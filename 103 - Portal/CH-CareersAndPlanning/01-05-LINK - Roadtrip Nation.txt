^^ LINK - Roadtrip Nation

[Roadtrip Nation](https://roadtripnation.com/) is a great resource where you can explore careers and pathways. There are interviews with people in  industry, a course you can take, and other cool tools to help you find a desirable career.

Of particular note is the [Roadmap tool](https://roadtripnation.com/roadmap) - in under thirty seconds you can narrow down some pathways that may not be on your radar.

By the way, this is reminiscent of a talk Elizabeth Gilbert (author of "Eat, Pray, Love") where she categorizes the different types of jobs:
* Hobby
* Job
* Career
* Vocation
