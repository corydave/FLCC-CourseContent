AI RESOURCES - Readings and Videos

Here are some AMAZING resources. Choose your own adventure... I'll recommend you check out at least two, but I won't stop you if you want to look at all of them!



  • AI: A Simple to Understand Guide - Google
    • Published April 8, 2024
    • Includes a video overview (22:01)
  • What is Prompt Engineering? - Google
    • Published August 16, 2023
    • Includes a video overview (1:53)
  • Getting Started with AI Prompting - Ethan Mollick
    • Published November 24, 2025
  • How People Use ChatGPT - Chatterji et al
    • Published September 15, 2025
    • This paper has 63 pages
      • At a minimum, I recommend jumping to Section 5.3 User Intent which begins on page 16. Look at the visuals!
  • Art and the science of generative AI: A deeper dive - Epstein et al
    • Published July 16, 2023
    • "As creativity is reimagined, so too may be many sectors of society. Understanding the impact of generative AI—and making policy decisions around it—requires new interdisciplinary scientific inquiry into culture, economics, law, algorithms, and the interaction of technology and creativity."
    • Poses many thinking questions on page 12
  • On the Dangers of Stochastic Parrots: Can Language Models Be Too Big? - Bender, Gebru, et al
    • Published March 3, 2021
      • That's over four years ago!
    • "We recall again Birhane and Prabhu’s [18] words (inspired by Ruha Benjamin [15]): 'Feeding AI systems on the world’s beauty, ugliness, and cruelty, but expecting it to reflect only the beauty is a fantasy.' " (Section 7)
    • Poses different topics to consider / potential implications on society
  • The economic potential of generative AI: The next productivity frontier - McKinsey & Company
    • Published June 14, 2023
    • Lots of economic potential / predictions discussed 
  • Generative artificial intelligence - Wikipedia
    • Updated throughout 2026 (most recent edit date at the bottom of the page!)
  • 2025 AI Index Report - AI Index Steering Committee at Stanford University
    • Published April 2025
