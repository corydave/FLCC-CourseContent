Description


There are so many things to think about with AI! Based on everything you've read, seen, listened to, or thought about so far during this chapter, respond to one or more of these questions:

  1. What do you think college will look like with respect to AI in 10 years?  What changes, if any, do you think higher learning institutions should make now in response to AI?
  2. What do you think the impact of AI will be on jobs over the next decade? What changes, if any, do you think employers should make now in response to AI?
  3. What do you think is the biggest potential positive impact of AI on society? What do you think is the biggest potential negative impact? Why?
