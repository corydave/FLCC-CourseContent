^^ READING - Chapter 4 - Standard Classes

The reading for this chapter is [Chapter 4 - Standard Classes](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1-FmWZFelbIoXxZuQifn5Xv1CceJwMijJnf7A3rjghOI//). In this chapter we'll start looking at some of the ways to potentiate your programs. We'll be looking at the proper way to get information from the user with the `Scanner` class. We'll look at some of the `Math` class functions. Be on the lookout for the subtle differences between the `Random` class and the `Math.random()` function in the `Math` class.

Spoiler alert:
* `Math.random()` gives an answer between `0.0` and `0.99999` whereas the `Random` class can be used to generate an `int` or a `double` - you choose! - as well as within a range.
