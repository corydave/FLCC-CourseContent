The reading for this chapter is [Chapter 3 - Math](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1yCS7eqh9uWHzliIbqoKRV_Z4I7pZ2kv8WR6rOMINaoY). In addition to the normal arithmetic operators (addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division) we will be exploring **modulus** (which is really like the remainder when dividing two numbers). 

We'll look at some ways to generate random numbers, **casting**, and overflow errors (which can be bad news for you because bad actors look for overflow errors as an entry point into your software!).
