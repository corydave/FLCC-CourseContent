^^ READING - Chapter 6 - Introduction to Selections

The reading for this chapter is [Chapter 6 - Complex Selections](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1xeEZH-k8q1ZcHQQGHGwwTD2E_TeDMXBxr0MGSEXqfj4/). In this chapter we'll dive deeper into`if` statements. We'll look at more complicated conditionals (instead of checking the state of one variable, we can check the state of multiple variables, for instance). We'll also look at the `switch` statement (which is easier to read but does the same thing as nested `if-else-if` statements.

```java

if (age >= 35 && country.equals("United States") {
  System.out.println("You are old enough to run for President.");
}

```
