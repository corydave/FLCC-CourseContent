# PRINTERS!
Hey folks - another short unit as we look towards break!!!! If you work in an IT capacity you will almost certainly find yourself troubleshooting printers. They are fickle machines with a lot of moving parts that have a small threshold for error. 

Have you ever thought about how a printer with duplex (printing on both sides) works? Do you know the difference between toner and ink? 

Buckle in. We're gonna look at how to emerge victorious when dealing with printers.

Oh yeah. Also, they are a pretty popular entry point for network intruders!
