I'm interested in what *you* think this should look like. There are no rules, no formats, no suggestions. Well, that's not true. I suggest that you experiment with AI if you'd like. Given your major, you will almost certainly be dealing with AI in your career. 

So take this opportunity to experiment with it. Design a manual. Make a study guide for the content in this course. Work with AI to do a slideshow. Make an interactive app (this one is easier than you think!). You can use AI for any (or all) of this. This project could involve comparing and contrasting services and prices from other competitors (Microsoft Azure and Google Cloud). You might write a scenario where you work at a company and are tasked with determining if cloud computing is appropriate for your company and, if so, providing a rational argument

One rule: You _have_ to be the "human in the lead". You can't just prompt AI once, get a response, and submit it. You need to vet the content. Make it your own.

Although you can use AI as much as you'd like, you do not have to use AI. You can do this without AI if you'd prefer.
