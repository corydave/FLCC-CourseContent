Hello and welcome to CSC 153! My name is Dave Ghidiu and I'm your instructor for this course, Introduction to Cloud Computing. I'm so looking forward to getting to know you better and learning about the cloud together. This course will serve as an introduction to cloud computing - specifically through the lens of Amazon Web Services. We'll also look at other computer nerdy things like *git*, *linux*, and a bunch of other tools that will equip you in your career.

There is no textbook that you need to buy for this class! Much of this class will be in the Amazon environment, and the rest of the topics will be covered in class.

You can reach me at dave.ghidiu@flcc.edu.

Below is also a sub-folder called **Course Information**. In this folder you'll find resources that might help you throughout the semester. This includes things like the invitation to Discord, a place to give me feedback, the Student Hours schedule, and some other cool things.

Take a look at the syllabus below to get an understanding of this course. You can *totally* email me if you have any questions!

I'm looking forward to learning with you this semester!
