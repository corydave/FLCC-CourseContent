Google Documents (often called "Google Docs") can be a very simple word processor, but there are a lot of great features in Docs that we'll dig into. One of the more potent - and lesser known - features is the ability to embed content from Google Drawings and Google Sheets. 

After exploring some of the cool features in Docs, we'll look at a few ways to really leverage Docs for designing assignments for our students.
