In addition to tools dedicated to help you make lesson plans, there are a ton of emerging tools that will help you create supporting assets (music, images, etc.) as you are designing your learning experiences. 

This lab explores some of the niche tools that exist today and can help you create cool, multi-modal experiences for your learners!

Tools we'll be looking at:

* [MusicFX](https://aitestkitchen.withgoogle.com/tools/music-fx) - Create ambient music for your lessons
* [ImageFX](https://aitestkitchen.withgoogle.com/tools/image-fx) - Granular control for adjusting AI images
* [GenType](https://labs.google/gentype) - Creating sweet, sweet custom fonts
* [TextFX](https://textfx.withgoogle.com/) - Generating a list of ideas topically related to a specific word or phrase
* [Illuminate](https://illuminate.google.com/home) - Distilling a research paper or book into an approachable podcast
