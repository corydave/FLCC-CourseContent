^^ PODCAST - Technical Writing 

The CodeNewbie Podcast is a great podcast to subscribe to. It contains stories from people on their coding journey. [Check out their website](https://www.codenewbie.org/podcast) and be sure to subscribe; the episodes are really insightful for aspiring software engineers.

In [December 2022, Philip Kiely sat down for an interview on the podcast and talked about *technical writing*](https://www.codenewbie.org/podcast/technical-writing-e3787c31-47c8-4bbe-83b4-ca58ff809e06). If you'd like the transcript, [click here](https://www.codenewbie.org/podcast/technical-writing-e3787c31-47c8-4bbe-83b4-ca58ff809e06/transcript).
