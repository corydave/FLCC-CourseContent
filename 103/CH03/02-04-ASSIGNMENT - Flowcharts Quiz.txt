Flowcharting is a language. Much like mathematicians worldwide agree that the plus sign means addition, the flowcharters worldwide agree (for example) that the diamond means *decision*.

Every shape in a flowchart has a specific meaning; you can't use whatever shapes you want. You *have* to use the proper shape to express your intended meaning.

This quiz assesses your understanding of the different shapes and their meanings (at least, six of the most popular shapes.

You need to score a 100% on the quiz to see the flowchart assignment.
