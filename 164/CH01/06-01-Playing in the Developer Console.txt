# Developer Console

The Developer Console is available on most browsers. In Chrome, hit *^key F12* to open it. In the console:
* You can use it to run any JavaScript
* You can use it to access anything in the DOM of the webpage
* You can use it as a makeshift calculator
* You can hit the *^key ↑* key to scroll through the previous commands (note that *^key ↓* will scroll the opposite way)
